Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2023

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us make the site work better for you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Game progress cookies: Save your game progress and scores

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser Session
Persistent Cookies Remain on your device until deleted or expired Up to 12 months

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. For more information about how to do this, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services may place cookies on your device. We have no control over these cookies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back regularly to stay informed of any updates.
